About

Walkthrough

  • Mar.2014 - Mar.2017

    UI/UX Design engineer in Vinyl Group

    I worked as an design research engineer at VINYL X, who is a service ·UX design consulting group that seeks to empathize and evolve through business as a business between business and customers. The value of the products that customers buy and experience today is driven by the services they provide for their products for a longer period of time beyond the product lifecycle. I ensure that the business direction of our company and our customers' needs are met successfully.

  • Nov.2012 - Feb.2013

    Online Marketing Analyst in Forward3d UK

    I run search and display marketing campaigns with industry-leading data science, technology and performance linguistics. My best practice implementation approach to technology. Today, I managed activity for a collection of ambitious global brands, running cross market and multilingual campaigns worldwide, especially for Korean markets.

  • Nov. 2012 - Nov. 2013

    Online Marketing Analyst in Wavemetrix UK

    My role was in helping clients understand what consumers are saying about a company, by analysing discussion in a way which will answer the questions. The unique combination of technology and accurate human coding allows me to extract those answers and deliver the relevant insights to you. I worked in any language and across all online sources.

  • Apr.2009 - Mar.2011

    Visual Designer & Marketer in Lookoptics

    I assure that high-quality promotion(BTL) for the 1st in eyewear distribution in Korea, ‘Lookoptics’ who is a wholesaler of photo equipments and optical products, eyeglass frames and sunglasses wholesale and retail distribution companies. In order to eliminate the distrust of the price of existing glasses customers, I emphasized on the first in the eyeglass industry, and popularized high quality fashion eyewear at reasonable prices, thereby expanding the scope of our customers not only for people with poor eyesight but also for everyone.
